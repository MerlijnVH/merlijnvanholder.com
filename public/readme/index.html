Hello! I’m Merlijn — a Belgian game developer and programmer who plays a lot of video games.

Professionally, I split my time between three companies I founded: Mithra Games, my one-person indie studio; Pandafish, a web development studio; and MyB’eats, an app and loyalty platform.

I’ve been tinkering with games since elementary school, building levels for Doom, Quake, Half-Life, and many more. Later, as a technical artist in the games industry. All in all I’ve worked on games for web, mobile, desktop and VR. These days I’m just doing my own thing with Godot.

Find me on Bluesky if you’d like to get in touch!
